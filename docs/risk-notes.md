# Risk Notes — On-chain Registry

## Risk level

Very high

## Primary risk notes

Tokenization, ownership ledgers, securities, property rights, compliance, and financial claims require legal review.

## Watch areas

- Payments
- Customer data
- Contact forms
- Legal/compliance claims
- Analytics/tracking
- Domain/DNS settings
- Regulated services
- Unsupported claims
- Insurance/licensing requirements
- Safety-sensitive services

## Rule

Pause for human approval before implementing R3 or R4 work.
