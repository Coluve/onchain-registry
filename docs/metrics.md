# Metrics — On-chain Registry

Track improvements by waypoint.

## Metric categories

- Clarity: Can a visitor understand the offer within 5 seconds?
- Conversion: Is there a clear contact or quote path?
- Trust: Are proof, service area, process, and credibility visible?
- SEO: Are title, description, headings, and local-service terms clear?
- Performance: Does the site remain fast and static-hosting friendly?
- Accessibility: Are headings, links, labels, and alt text usable?
- Maintainability: Is the repo easier to update?
- Operational readiness: Is the service manually sellable?

## Current baseline

Unknown unless analytics or business records are provided.

## Measurement notes

Do not invent analytics. If no analytics are available, use predicted metric impact and mark it as a hypothesis.
