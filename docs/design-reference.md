# Design Reference — On-Chain Registry

## Design objective

Build a fast, static, GitHub Pages-compatible website that positions On-Chain Registry as a clear, credible, educational, compliance-aware market insights and investment research platform.

## Visual direction

- Clear, credible, educational, modern, trust-focused, research-oriented, accessible, responsive, and calm.
- Use restrained institutional colors: charcoal, slate, soft blue, muted gold, white panels, and light paper backgrounds.
- Avoid hype-heavy crypto styling, neon gradients, urgent trading motifs, and dashboards that look executable.
- Keep the hero contained on desktop and mobile.
- Keep navigation readable across the full market-research page set.

## Required public sections

Homepage:

1. Hero
2. About — “A Clearer Way to Explore Market Ideas”
3. What You Can Do on On-Chain Registry
4. Brand Story — “From Market Noise to Market Clarity”
5. Market Overview — “Follow What’s Moving”
6. Insights and Analysis — “Research Before You React”
7. Portfolio Tracking — “Keep Your Market View Organized”
8. Learning Center — “Build Market Knowledge Step by Step”
9. Community Forum — “Discuss Ideas. Learn From Others.”
10. Trust and Risk Disclosure — “Built for Informed Research”

Navigation:

- Home
- Markets
- Insights
- Portfolio
- Learn
- Community
- Tokenization
- Trust & Risk

## Safety rules

Do not add:

- Forms, payments, accounts, uploads, analytics, tracking pixels, dashboards, APIs, broker/exchange/wallet connections, or backend logic.
- Personalized advice, financial planning, investment recommendations, legal advice, tax advice, securities advice, compliance claims, or custody claims.
- AI market predictions, accurate forecasts, trade signals, trade mirroring, expert consultations, premium strategies, sponsored-finance core features, data monetization, or hype-heavy crypto claims.
- Token issuance, registry services, ownership records, securities offerings, tokenization functionality, or claims of legal/compliance effect.
