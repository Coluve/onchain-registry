Trust and risk disclosure

Built for Informed Research

On-Chain Registry is designed as a research, education, and organization concept. It is not a financial advisor, broker, exchange, tax advisor, legal advisor, trade-signal service, prediction product, or tokenization service.

Research only

Public content should help users organize questions and learn concepts.

  • Educational market context.
  • General asset comparison prompts.
  • Watchlist organization.
  • Structured discussion topics.

Not advice

Users should not rely on the site for personal decisions.

  • No personalized financial, legal, tax, compliance, securities, or investment advice.
  • No transaction, allocation, or timing recommendations.
  • No individualized advisory services.
  • No expert appointment marketplace.

No prediction posture

The site avoids guaranteed-sounding language.

  • No accurate-forecast claims.
  • No AI market prediction claims.
  • No trade mirroring.
  • No premium investment strategies.

No operational tokenization

Tokenization is discussed only as a research topic.

  • No token issuance.
  • No ownership registry.
  • No securities or compliance coverage claims.
  • No customer onboarding or data collection on the static site.
